What is the IRS?

Internal Revenue Serice is a government agency that collets federal tax from U.S taxpayers which mainly includes individuals and corporations. It is a division of the U.S. Treasury Department that performs three main functions:

  • 1. Tax return processing
  • 2. Taxpayer services
  • 3. Enforcement of federal tax laws

Why is tax so important?

  • 1. Funds public services: Government constructs the parameters for everyday life for citizens, funds necessary services, maintains law, and protects citizens from outlaws.
  • 2. Redistributes wealth: Taxes can be used to redistribute wealth from the wealthy to the less fortunate.
  • 3. Encourages economic growth: Tax policies can also be used to encourage economic growth.
  • 4. Regulates behavior: taxes on cigarettes and alcohol are meant to discourage people from consuming these harmful products.
