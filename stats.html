Tax Collection Stats (Recent)

During Fiscal Year (FY) 2021, the IRS collected more than $4.1 trillion in gross taxes, processed more than 261 million tax returns and other forms, and issued more than $1.1 trillion in tax refunds

It's worth noting that the COVID-19 pandemic and related economic stimulus measures implemented by the U.S. gov ernment have had a significant impact on tax collection statistics over the past few years. For example, the IRS extended the tax filing deadline for the 2020 tax year, and the federal government implemented several rounds of economic stimulus payments that were not subject to federal income tax.
